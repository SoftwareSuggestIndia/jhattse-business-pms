Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hotel management software

What is Hotel Management Software (PMS)? +

Hotel Management Software (PMS) helps manage bookings, reservations, guest records, billing, housekeeping, and revenue operations in hotels. It centralizes daily operations and improves guest experience through automation and digital tools. Jhattse Business PMS includes features like channel manager integration, booking engine, WhatsApp API, and GST-compliant billing specifically designed for Indian hotels.

What is a channel manager and why do I need it? +

A channel manager synchronizes your room availability and pricing across multiple OTAs such as Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, Airbnb, Goibibo, and others to prevent overbooking and maximize occupancy. It eliminates manual work in updating room inventory and pricing across different platforms, saving you time and preventing costly booking errors.

Does Jhattse Business include WhatsApp API integration? +

Yes! Jhattse Business Hotel PMS includes built-in WhatsApp Business API integration for sending automated check-in confirmations, check-out reminders, invoices, payment links, and promotional messages. This helps you enhance guest communication and improve customer experience while reducing manual work for your staff.

Which OTAs are integrated with Jhattse Business? +

Jhattse Business integrates with 100+ OTAs including major platforms like Booking.com, MakeMyTrip (MMT), Goibibo, Agoda, Yatra, Expedia, Airbnb, Google Hotels, EaseMyTrip, Via.com, and many more. All integrations provide real-time sync of rates and availability to prevent overbooking.

Is there a free trial available? +

Yes! Jhattse Business provides a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You can explore all features and see how our hotel management software works for your property. We also offer free demos where our team will walk you through the platform and answer all your questions.

What kind of hotels can use this software? +

Jhattse Business is suitable for all types of accommodation businesses - from small boutique hotels and B&Bs with just a few rooms to large hotels and multi-property chains. Whether you take bookings from multiple OTAs or manage everything manually, our PMS can help you digitalize operations, streamline billing, manage guest information, and track revenue efficiently.
